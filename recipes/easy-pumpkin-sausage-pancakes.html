Easy Pumpkin Sausage Pancakes

Description

These easy pumpkin sausage pancakes are a fall breakfast treat! Fully cooked sausage patties are dipped in pumpkin pancake batter, then griddled until golden, and served with syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pancake mix
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 (8 count) packages fully cooked sausage patties, such as Jimmy DeanÂ®
  • 1 cup syrup, or as needed for serving

Steps

  1. Add pancake mix, pumpkin, and milk to a bowl and stir until well combined.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Working in batches, dip each sausage patty into pancake batter, and cook in the skillet until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  3. Serve immediately with syrup or refrigerate until ready to serve.
